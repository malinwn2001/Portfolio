Personal Interests

In the dynamic realm of creativity, two passions have been my guiding lights: video editing and video games.

For the past eight years, video editing has been my chosen avenue for unrestricted self-expression.

It's not just about splicing clips; it's about pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling

and exploring the far reaches of my imagination.


From the earliest tentative edits to the seamless compositions of today, each project has become a canvas for my artistic evolution.

Beyond the pixels and timelines, I find solace in the captivating world of video games.

It's my sanctuary — a place where I can momentarily silence the analytical side of my brain and immerse myself in

the narratives and interactive experiences that only games can offer. Whether embarking on epic quests or discovering hidden gems in the indie gaming scene, each game presents a new adventure waiting to be explored.


This dual passion for video editing and gaming brings a unique balance to my life.

Video editing allows me to shape narratives and craft visual stories, while gaming permits me to be a participant in stories crafted by others.

Together, they create a harmonious blend of creation and consumption in my creative universe.
